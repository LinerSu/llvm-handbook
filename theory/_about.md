# theory/ — definitions & proofs (tool-agnostic)

Reusable mathematical foundations: lattices, Galois connections, fixpoints, collecting/abstract semantics, the monotone framework, abstract domains (intervals, octagons, polyhedra, congruences). A note belongs here when its subject is a **definition or theorem**, independent of any compiler. Many concept notes link *into* this layer. See [[classification-protocol]].
